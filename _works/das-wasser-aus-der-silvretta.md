Dimensions (cms): 50 X 31 (approx) Video (loop) projection  

As artist in residence: Kunsthaus Klosters - 800 years of Klosters  

Special thanks to Helmut Weissert and the team of Kunsthaus Klosters

Installation View: Kunsthaus Klosters, Landstrasse 173, 7250 Klosters-Serneus, Switzerland - 2022

Bündnerschiefer, a metamorphic shale from the Engadine window north of Scuol. The rock was a sediment 130 million years ago, it was metamorphosed during mountain building and now it is a micaschist (micas are platy minerals in the rock).
