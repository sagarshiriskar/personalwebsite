**Feature documentary (63’)** • 4K • English & Hebrew • Swiss production

This film follows Kereni, a 9-year-old girl with quadriplegic cerebral palsy, who communicates through an eye gaze-controlled computer and dreams of meeting the Dalai Lama.

The story traces her family’s journey to India as they attempt to fulfill her wish, navigating the complexities of travelling with a child with severe physical disabilities in India.
